## What is lorem ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is meaningless placeholder text that stands in for real copy while a design comes together. Because the words carry no meaning, they let you see how a layout reads — its spacing, line length, and rhythm — without anyone getting pulled into editing the content. This generator produces as much or as little as you need, by paragraph, sentence, or word, entirely in your browser.

## Why designers and developers use placeholder text

Real copy rarely arrives before the layout does. Placeholder text fills that gap:

- **Test the layout, not the words** — check how headings, cards, and body text flow at realistic length.
- **Avoid premature feedback** — reviewers comment on the design instead of rewriting sentences.
- **Fill components and mockups** — drop believable text into a card, a table row, or a wireframe in seconds.

The point is a neutral gray box of text that looks like content without being content.

## How to use the generator

1. Choose a unit — **paragraphs**, **sentences**, or **words**.
2. Set **how many** to match the space you're filling.
3. Keep the classic **“Lorem ipsum…”** opening or turn it off for a fully random start.
4. Hit **Regenerate** for a different batch, then copy it into your mockup.

## When to stop using lorem ipsum

Placeholder text is for the draft, not the ship. Swap it for real copy before launch — final wording changes line counts and spacing, and shipping lorem ipsum by accident is a well-known, embarrassing bug. Treat it as scaffolding you remove once the real content is in.
